System Validation & FAT Protocol

This environment executes the Tier-1 IEC 61131-3 logic engine for a high-pressure 3-Phase Separator via a JavaScript port. It strictly enforces IEC 61511 Functional Safety, ISA-106 Procedural Automation, and ISA-18.2 Alarm Management. Utilize the checklists below to conduct a comprehensive Factory Acceptance Test (FAT).

01 ISA-106 Deterministic Startup

Validates sequential state logic, ensuring purged initialization and safe pressurization prior to BPCS handover.

  • Verify Master Trip is latched upon Black-Start. Ensure ZSC (Closed) is checked, then click Reset Trip.
  • Click Start Plant. Verify system enters State 1 (Purging).
  • Decrease PT sliders to 0.0 Bar. Verify transition to State 2. When the SDV turns Green, immediately check ZSO (Open).
  • Increase PT sliders > 45.0 Bar. Verify transition to State 3 (Running) and BPCS PID activation.

02 BPCS Split-Range Pressure Control

Verifies economic process control and dynamic safety flare venting prior to an SIS Trip constraint.

  • In State 3, vary PT sliders between 45.0 and 52.0 Bar. Verify Sales Gas PCV dynamically modulates accordingly.
  • Increase PT above 52.0 Bar. Verify Sales PCV pins at 100% and Emerg. Flare PCV turns green and ramps open to prevent an SIS Trip.

03 BPCS Anti-Blow-By & Plausibility Clamps

Ensures physical plausibility clamping and validates overrides to prevent catastrophic gas blow-by into liquid lines.

  • Anti-Blow-By: Decrease LT (Total Level) < 5.0%. Verify Oil and Water valves are systematically overridden to 0% (Red) to prevent gas escape.
  • Physics Clamp: Attempt to drag Water Interface higher than Total Level. Verify logic mathematically clamps Interface to <= Total Level.

04 IEC 61511 Safety Matrix Degradation

Demonstrates dynamic functional safety matrix shifting from 2oo3 to a highly sensitive 1oo2 architecture.

  • Uncheck LINK on the PT matrix to simulate independent sensor deviation.
  • Check the MOS Bypass for PT A, OR drag PT A all the way right to simulate a hardware short-circuit.
  • Verify PT Matrix safely degrades to 1oo2 Degraded without tripping the facility.
  • Drag PT B above 55.0 Bar. Verify the 1oo2 matrix mathematically catches the localized hazard and trips the plant.

05 Dynamic Bypass Veto (Safety Override)

Validates that active Maintenance Override Switches (MOS) are systematically revoked during critical hazard spikes.

  • Reset plant to State 3. Ensure vessel pressure is < 49.0 Bar. Activate MOS Bypass for PT A.
  • Simulate process hazard: Drag PT B and PT C to 50.0 Bar (> 90% of trip limit).
  • Verify the red VETO alert activates, automatically shredding the human bypass request to ensure full matrix protection.

06 APM Transit Watchdog & Trip Latching

Evaluates predictive maintenance stroke timers, limit switch discrepancy tracking, and ESD latch memory.

  • Click ESD Stop to initiate trip. Uncheck ZSO and check ZSC (Closed) to clear advisor.
  • Reset to State 1, then lower pressure to enter State 2. SDV will command Open (Green).
  • Transit Watchdog: Do not check the ZSO limit switch. Wait exactly 15 seconds.
  • Verify the APM Watchdog registers a STUCK VALVE, triggering an immediate Master SIS Trip.
